The Science of Acoustic Mitigation

The effectiveness of acoustic techniques lies in their ability to target Quagga and Zebra mussels at various stages of their life cycle. For instance, ultrasonic frequencies can cause physical damage to the larvae, preventing them from maturing into adults. At the settlement stage, low and middle frequency vibrations can deter mussels from attaching onto surfaces. For adult mussels, low frequency sound waves can potentially reduce their reproduction and activity.
